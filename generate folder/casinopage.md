Are online casinos safe and fair? 
Licensed, regulated, and certified online casinos are completely safe and fair to play at. UKGC and MGA are some of the most trustworthy licenses. When it comes to certifications, look for the eCOGRA, iTechLabs, or TST badges on the casino site or in our dedicated review page before playing.

Can I win real money on online casinos? 
Yes, you can win real money when playing on an online casino as long as you make real-money bets as opposed to playing the games in demo mode. 
Can I gamble online for free? 
Yes, you can gamble online for free either by claiming no deposit bonuses or simply by playing games in demo mode. The demo mode for online casino games means all bets and winnings are fictive. 

We have a variety of games in the Play for Coins section, a special place where you can play slot machines for free using your Coins. They can later be exchanged for all sorts of rewards (including real money prizes).

Can a casino refuse to pay out? 
Yes, an online casino can refuse to pay out your winnings. This may happen if you have unknowingly breached the terms and conditions or you are not yet eligible to request withdrawals from your account. 

This may also happen on some unlicensed and untrustworthy casinos for no apparent reason and without giving you many explanations. That is why we strongly advise you to only gamble at trusted casinos.
